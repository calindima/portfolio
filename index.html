About me

Hey! My name is Calin Dima and I'm a 25 year old Romanian living and studying in Copenhagen. Before moving, I was studying law and working as a waiter when I decided to make a change and embark on a journey.
I owe all of it to my passion for IT and the marvels of the Internet. Now, I'm enrolled in my 3rd semester at KEA, studying Multimedia Design and Communication and continuing to pursue my interest in Web Development.
Of course, if you work hard, you should also play hard, so I love hanging out with my friends, playing video games and watching movies.

In the last year and a half I have learned to make the web languages to work together to form the front-end design of an website by applying information that affects content, style and interactivity.
I have built several products from scratch using different technologies such as HTML5 to structure the content, CSS Flexbox/CSS Grid/MaterializeCSS to add styling, SVG's for animations and Vanilla JavaScript to add interactivity.
